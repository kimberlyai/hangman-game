How to Play Hangman

📖 Game Rules

  1. The computer picks a secret word from your chosen category
  2. You have 6 wrong guesses before the game ends
  3. Click on letters to guess them
  4. Correct guesses reveal the letter in the word
  5. Wrong guesses add a body part to the hangman drawing

🎮 Game Features

  • 💡 Hints: Get helpful clues about the word (limited per game)
  • 👁️ Reveal: Automatically reveal one letter (limited per game)
  • 📊 Statistics: Track your wins, losses, and win rate

💡 Tips & Strategies

  • Start with common vowels (A, E, I, O, U)
  • Save your hints for difficult words
